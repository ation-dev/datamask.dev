Work with us#

Datamask is being built in close collaboration with a small number of design partners.

If you are responsible for:

  • QA or UAT environments
  • test data management
  • data security in non-production systems
  • or developer experience around realistic test data

we would love to talk.

What it means to be a design partner#

Design partners help shape Datamask by:

  • sharing real schemas or environments
  • describing how non-production data is used today
  • running early versions of the engine
  • and giving direct feedback on usability and data quality

In return, you get:

  • early access to Datamask
  • influence over its roadmap
  • and a tool shaped around your real workflows

This is a technical partnership, not a sales process.

What we are looking for#

The best design partners are teams that:

  • rely on production-like data in QA or UAT
  • have strict security or compliance constraints
  • and are frustrated with copying production data or using unstable synthetic datasets

How to get in touch#

If this resonates, email us at partners@datamask with:

  • a short description of your current test-data workflow
  • the kind of systems you run
  • and what you struggle with today

We will respond immediately.

Datamask is still early, by design.

If you want to help define what safe, reproducible non-production data should look like, this is the right time to be involved.
